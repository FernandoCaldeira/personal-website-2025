Product Manager — Innovation Lab, Tinder SELECT, Profile, Chat & Connections

2022 – Present

We own "Big Bets" from A to Z, spearheading game-changing features across Chat, Profile, and Discovery. Once we find product-market fit and scale the initial impact, we transition ownership to core teams for long-term optimization.

  • Tackling Gen Z Engagement: Spearheaded the "Double Date" initiative and new discovery frameworks to reverse declining usage trends, focusing on shared experiences rather than just swiping.
  • Scaling Premium Value (Tinder SELECT): Orchestrated product enhancements for the high-end segment, directly increasing retention and premium revenue through targeted, high-touch user experiences.
  • High-Impact Feature Validation: Focused on rapid experimentation to identify sustainable engagement drivers, successfully scaling innovations that were later integrated into the core product ecosystem.
  • Design-Led Profile Optimization: Launched a suite of profile features that led to record photo uploads and discovery depth.

  • Trusted Execution: Maintained role through layoffs—delivering confidence and consistent results.

Ablo

Product Manager — Monetization & Growth

2020 – 2022

Ablo connected people worldwide with real-time chat, video, and live translations. It was named Google App of the Year in its first year. I was responsible for transforming a viral sensation (50M+ users) into a sustainable business, ensuring that revenue growth never compromised the "magic" of serendipitous global connection.

  • Aggressive Monetization Strategy: Engineered a new revenue roadmap that delivered a 300% increase in quarterly revenue. I achieved this by balancing new subscription tiers with gamified micro-transactions.
  • Strategic Feature Pruning: To sharpen retention, I proposed and executed the removal of low-value legacy features, reallocating resources to a new streaming vertical that drove higher user LTV (Lifetime Value).
  • Scale from Zero to 50M: Navigated the challenges of hyper-growth, working closely with data teams to identify and double down on the core features that moved Ablo from a niche tool to a global platform. During my stay I've created +150 specs.

This role gave me my first managerial responsibilities and shaped how I approach problem framing, solution design, and data analysis/impact measurement.

Linkuid

Co-Founder — Product & Design

2023 — 2024

Built alongside my role at Tinder, Linkuid was born from a personal frustration with development bottlenecks. I co-founded this platform to automate staging environments platform to speed up software development by providing instantly available, isolated environments.

  • Product-Led Infrastructure: Defined the vision for a staging-automation tool that integrated Docker and GitHub, turning a complex DevOps workflow into a one-click experience for developers.
  • Full-Cycle Ownership: From initial user research and Figma prototyping to managing the deployment of isolated environments, I acted as the bridge between technical feasibility and market need.
